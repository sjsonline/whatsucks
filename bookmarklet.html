Installing a 'What Sucks' Bookmarklet on an iOS Device

This bookmarklet will allow users to easily submit a link to the This is What Sucks website.

Just drag the following button to your toolbar.

What Sucks

The 'What Sucks' Bookmarklet lets you grab images and videos from around the web and add them to the This is What Sucks website. Just click the bookmark when you see something you want to pin!

1. Bookmark this page

Tap the middle icon below, then tap Add Bookmark, then tap Save.

2. Select the text and copy it
